STRONG NATION


Combining body weight, muscle strength and cardio movements synchronized with music that is specifically designed to fit each movement, Strong Zumba is a great exercise to help you reach your weight loss goals faster, build muscle mass, increase strength, and maintain a healthy heart.

WORKOUT


It is a combination of resistance exercises and burning exercises, which consists of different exercises using time for the body's endurance and improving physical fitness


RPM


It is a group cycling exercise where you can control its intensity It is fun and low impact and you can burn up to 600 calories in a session With the wonderful sound of music, your coach takes you on a trip to climb hills and run fast and flat.

BODY SCULPT


Or the so-called body sculpting, which is a set of exercises to tighten the muscles of the body using weights, belts, bars, weights and various tools, which gives you strength with muscle tone and reduces bone loss


BODY PUMP


It consists of a set of resistance exercises that exhaust muscles using light weights with high repetitions, and it is the secret to developing skinny sports muscles, burning 560 calories within 50 minutes. It also helps build lean muscles, which increases the body's ability to burn calories in the long run.

BODY COMBAT


Inspired by the art of fighting and self-defense, it uses high energy from the body to improve fitness and burn calories up to 740 calories.


BODY RESISTANCE

ZUMBA


It depends on the diversity and merging of many sports and dances such as salsa, samba and belly dance. Zumba exercises achieve a faster loss of stored fat in the body for its ability to burn calories.


YOGA


It is a mind and body sport, a physical exercise that helps to communicate with oneself through breathing, helps to increase flexibility, and improves heart health. It improves mood and helps reduce stress
